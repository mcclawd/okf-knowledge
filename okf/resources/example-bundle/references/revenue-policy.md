# Revenue recognition policy

Pointer to the authoritative finance policy that defines when an order's value counts as
recognized revenue. The [gross revenue](/metrics/gross-revenue.md) metric and the
[revenue reconciliation](/playbooks/revenue-reconciliation.md) playbook both follow this
policy.

This concept intentionally **references** the external document via `resource` rather than
reproducing it — see the OKF principle that a concept points to authoritative sources instead
of re-encoding them.
